My city

I'm from Kobe, where is famous for Kobe beef.
Kobe is the sixth-largest city in Japan and is the capital city of Hyogo
Prefecture. It is located on the southern side of the main island of Honshu,
on the north shore of Osaka Bay and about 30 km (19 mi) west of Osaka. With a
population around 1.5 million, the city is part of the Keihanshin
metropolitan area along with Osaka and Kyoto.

About Kobe beef

Kobe beef refers to beef from the Tajima strain of Wagyu cattle, raised in
Japan's Hyogo Prefecture according to rules as set out by the Kobe Beef
Marketing and Distribution Promotion Association. The meat is a delicacy
renowned for its flavor, tenderness, and fatty, well-marbled texture. Kobe beef
can be prepared as steak, sukiyaki, shabu shabu, sashimi, and teppanyaki.
